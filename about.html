About Me

Nina is a visual communicator, storyteller, textile artist and illustrator. 

If observing were a job she’d take it. She bases her art on experiences and inner dialogues, the smaller things she notices and the parts of conversations she'd catch.

Her need to better understand the world and give others a new way to look at things is what drives her work.

Currently based in Rotterdam focusing her work on textile art in the form of tufting, she's inspired and excited to see where this medium will bring her.
